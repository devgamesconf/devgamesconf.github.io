Rome, June 5-6, 2025

Italian Conference on Game Development

/dev/games/2025

The Italian Conference on Game Development will be held at the Conference Room of the University of Roma Tre, in the DICITA Department of Engineering.

Via Vito Voltrerra, 60, 00146 Rome, Italy

How to get there:

Public transportation: take the subway Metro B, get off at Stazione Basilica San Paolo and walk for 10 minutes.

With the car: take the Via del Mare exit (number 28) of GRA, towards the center, stay on Via Ostiense and turn left for Viale Guglielmo Marconi, then turn left on Via Corrado Segre and find a parking spot.
