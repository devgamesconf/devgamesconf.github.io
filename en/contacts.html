Italian Conference on Game Development


Our vision is to create a cornerstone in the Italian game development landscape: a place for exchanging new ideas, making new friends, and staying up-to-date with emerging technologies. A space where future game developers can begin to pursue their dreams.

Professionals

Whether you're an AAA or Indie developer, you'll feel at home at /dev/games. Stay up-to-date with new techniques and connect with key players in development, art, and the gaming business.

Educators and Students

If you teach or study subjects related to game development, this is the conference for you. It's your chance to meet and speak with the people who turn dreams into works of art.

Businesses

Come to show your products and to meet new potential talents for your company. You will have the chance to find a space to meet the right people to grow your business in the world of game development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Italian Conference on Game Development

  • Why attend?

    The conference is dedicated to the world of game development in its entirety: development, art, and business. Come to connect with professionals from this vast industry.

  • Just for Pros?

    Absolutely not! We welcome people of all experience levels, from seasoned professionals to students eager to start an exciting career. If you already teach, or will be teaching a related field, your're at home!

  • Accepting sponsors?

    Certainly. We do accept sponsors connected to the world of game development and, in a more general view, entertainment and computing. Contact us for more information.

  • Code of Conduct?

    Of course. The goal of /dev/games is to create a safe, inclusive, and welcoming space for everyone. For this reason, we have a Code of Conduct that we will adhere to, and we encourage everyone to show mutual respect.
