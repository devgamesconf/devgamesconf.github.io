Rome, June 25, 2024

Italian Conference on Game Development

/dev/games/2024

The Italian Conference on Game Development will be held at the Aula Magna of the University of Roma Tre, in the Department of Humanities.

Via Ostiense, 234, 00146 Rome, Italy

How to get there:

Public transportation: take the subway Metro B, get off at Stazione Marconi and walk for about 150 meters.

With the car: take the Via del Mare exit (number 28) of GRA, towards the center, and stay on Via Ostiense.
